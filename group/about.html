The NBHD

The Neighbourhood was established in August of 2011, from Newbury park, california. There are currently 4 members in the neighbourhood consisting of: Jesse Rutherford, Jeremy Freedman, Zach Abels, and Mikey Margott. Jesse Rutherford is the lead singer/vocalist. Jeremy Freedman is the guitarist and background vocalist. Zach Abels is the other guitarist in the band. And lastly, Mikey Margott is the bass guitarist.
